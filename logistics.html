Ferry Strategy

⚠️ Structural Change: Horseshoe Bay → Nanaimo

All vehicle travel from Horseshoe Bay to Nanaimo (Departure Bay) must now be booked and paid in advance. Drive-up fares are no longer available in this direction. This is due to multi-year terminal construction reducing staging space by 25%. This is not just "book early" — it is a fundamentally different fare structure.

Backup: If unavailable, Tsawwassen → Duke Point (Nanaimo) still offers drive-up and reservation fares.

Three "Watch Carefully" Days

Day 16 · 3 Aug
⚠️ Whistler → Horseshoe Bay → Ferry → Nanaimo → Cowichan

Only works with an early sailing and minimal stops. Don't dawdle leaving Whistler. Ferry is mandatory booking.

Day 24 · 11 Aug
⚠️ Tofino → Comox (~300 km)

Longest road day. Highway 4 is the single chokepoint across the Island with periodic closures. Check DriveBC the morning of travel.

Day 26 · 13 Aug
⚠️ Comox → Powell River → Saltery Bay → Earls Cove → Madeira Park

Double-ferry day. A missed Saltery Bay connection cascades into a very long day. Build generous buffers at every stage.
