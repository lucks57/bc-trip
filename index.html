The Route

Days 1–4

Downtown Vancouver

Three car-free days: Stanley Park Seawall, Granville Island, Science World. Acclimatise, explore, find your bearings.

19–22 July · No car

Days 5–12

Deep Cove

Nine nights in a cottage at the foot of the mountains. Quarry Rock, kayaking, Lynn Canyon, Lighthouse Park. Slow days on the deck.

22–31 July · 9 nights

Days 13–16

Sea-to-Sky & Whistler

Shannon Falls, Sea to Sky Gondola, Peak 2 Peak, Vallea Lumina night walk. River of Golden Dreams. Parkhurst ghost town.

31 July – 3 Aug · 3 nights · ~145 km

Days 17–26

Vancouver Island

Victoria, Butchart Gardens, whale watching. Cathedral Grove. Tofino surf and beaches. Bear watching. Cumberland ghost town. 10 nights, south to north.

3–13 Aug · 10 nights · ~900 km

Days 27–30

Sunshine Coast

Family time in Pender Harbour. Kayaking, freshwater lakes, Francis Point, Pender Hill. Four slow days before the final stretch.

13–17 Aug · 4 nights

Days 31–33

Vancouver Wind-Down

Three final nights in an apartment. Return car, last shopping, Kitsilano Beach, ice cream on the seawall. Fly out 20 August.

17–20 Aug · 3 nights
