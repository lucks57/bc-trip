Day-by-Day Itinerary

33 days across 6 segments — with distances, insider tips, and energy ratings

Energy Rating Guide

Each day has a coloured left border: ■ Light · ■ Medium · ■ Heavy — plus any fixed booking commitments marked with 🔒.

1

Downtown Vancouver

19–22 July · No car · 3 nights in hotel

Day 1 · Sat 19 July
Arrival
—

Arrive Vancouver. Transfer to downtown hotel. Rest, explore the neighbourhood. Jet lag day — keep it gentle.

Day 2 · Sun 20 July
Stanley Park & Seawall
0 km (walking)

Morning walk or bike along the Stanley Park Seawall (~9 km loop, flat). Vancouver Aquarium if the girls are keen. Afternoon at English Bay Beach.

Day 3 · Mon 21 July
Granville Island & False Creek
0 km (transit)

Aquabus ferry to Granville Island. Public Market, Kids Market, artisan shops. Afternoon: Science World or SeaBus to Lonsdale Quay.

Day 4 · Tue 22 July
Pick up car → Deep Cove
~20 km 🔒

Pick up rental car. Drive to Deep Cove cottage. Settle in, groceries in North Vancouver. Evening walk to Panorama Park and first doughnuts at Honey.

2

Deep Cove Cottage

22–31 July · 9 nights · Your North Shore base

Day 5 · Wed 23 July
Quarry Rock & Village Day
0 km (walking)

Hike Quarry Rock (3.8 km return, ~1.5 hrs) — stunning viewpoint over Indian Arm. Afternoon kayaking from the village waterfront.

Day 6 · Thu 24 July
Lynn Canyon
~15 km

Free suspension bridge, easy forest trails, swimming holes at 30 Foot Pool. Ecology Centre for the girls. Relaxed afternoon at the cottage.

Day 7 · Fri 25 July
Takaya Tours or Slow Day
0–10 km

Insider option: Guided canoe experience with Takaya Tours, led by Tsleil-Waututh Nation guides. More distinctive than a standard kayak rental. Alternative: cottage day — beach, paddleboarding, Baden Powell Trail.

Day 8 · Sat 26 July
Capilano or Grouse Mountain
~20 km

Option A: Capilano Suspension Bridge Park (Treetops Adventure, Cliffwalk). Option B: Grouse Mountain Skyride, alpine views, Lumberjack Show. Half-day; afternoon free.

Day 9 · Sun 27 July
Maplewood Flats & Vancouver
~40 km return

Morning: Maplewood Conservation Area — go early for best birding. 230+ species recorded, ~3 km easy boardwalk. Afternoon: Museum of Anthropology at UBC, Kitsilano Beach, or Granville Island.

Day 10 · Mon 28 July
Lighthouse Park
~40 km return

Easy coastal trails through old-growth forest to Point Atkinson Lighthouse (~5 km of paths). Rocky shoreline, tide pools. Quiet and beautiful.

Day 11 · Tue 29 July
Slow Day
0 km

Rest day at Deep Cove. Beach, small shops, evening paddling as sunset comes late (~9:15 pm in July).

Day 12 · Wed 30 July
Whytecliff Park & Prep Day
~50 km return

If tides are favourable: Whytecliff Park near Horseshoe Bay — marine protected area, excellent tide pools. Previews the ferry area for tomorrow. Afternoon: pack up, laundry, prep for mountains.

🌿 North Shore Insider Tips

Go to Maplewood early for best birding. Do Takaya Tours for the single most distinctive North Shore memory. Use Whytecliff only if tides are favourable and no one is tired.

3

Sea-to-Sky & Whistler

31 July – 3 Aug · 3 nights · Mountains, lakes, forest light shows

Day 13 · Thu 31 July
Deep Cove → Whistler (via Squamish)
~145 km

Sea-to-Sky Highway. Shannon Falls (5-min walk to 335 m waterfall). Sea to Sky Gondola — Spirit Trail loop, Sky Pilot Suspension Bridge. See the Stawamus Chief from below. Continue to Whistler. Evening: Purebread Bakery in Function Junction (off-peak for shorter queue).

Day 14 · Fri 1 Aug
Peak 2 Peak, River of Golden Dreams & Parkhurst
~15 km local

Morning: Peak 2 Peak Gondola — spectacular, no hiking required. Afternoon: Paddle the River of Golden Dreams — 5 km through wetlands from Alta Lake to Green Lake. Optional: Hike or kayak to Parkhurst ghost town on Green Lake — 1920s logging village reclaimed by forest. Atmospheric but optional.

Day 15 · Sat 2 Aug
Lakes, Culture & Vallea Lumina
~10 km local 🔒

Morning: Brandywine Falls. Midday: Squamish Lil'wat Cultural Centre — First Nations culture, craft workshops. Afternoon: Lakeside Park, Alta Lake. Train Wreck Trail (2 km, graffiti boxcars). Evening: Vallea Lumina — 1.5 km multimedia night walk through enchanted forest. Starts ~10 pm. Book ahead.

Day 16 · Sun 3 Aug ⚠️
Whistler → Vancouver Island
~225 km + ferry 🔒

⚠️ Watch carefully. Early start. Drive to Horseshoe Bay (~2 hrs). Ferry to Nanaimo (~1h40). Drive south to Cowichan. Overnight Chemainus or Duncan. Ferry booking is mandatory — drive-up not available.

🏔️ Whistler Insider Tips

Replace village wandering with River of Golden Dreams if the girls enjoy water. Treat Parkhurst as optional. Purebread off-peak. Vallea Lumina is the single most magical evening — prioritise it.

4

Vancouver Island

3–13 Aug · 10 nights · South to north: Victoria → Tofino → Comox

Day 17 · Mon 4 Aug
Cowichan → Victoria
~65 km

Cowichan Valley: Forest Discovery Centre or Merridale Cidery. Drive to Victoria. Inner Harbour: Fisherman's Wharf, Legislature Building, Beacon Hill Park petting zoo.

Day 18 · Tue 5 Aug
Victoria: Whales, Butchart & Gonzales Hill
~45 km 🔒

Morning: Whale watching (orcas, humpbacks, sea lions). Afternoon: Butchart Gardens (late afternoon for best light). Evening: Gonzales Hill Observatory — highest point on Victoria's south coast, panoramic views. Or walk Dallas Road waterfront.

Day 19 · Wed 6 Aug
Victoria → Parksville
~155 km

Drive north. Stop at Coombs — goats on the roof. Continue to Parksville. Rathtrevor Beach at low tide — extraordinary sand flats, warm pools. Build beach timing around low tide, not just weather.

Day 20 · Thu 7 Aug
Little Qualicum Falls & Cathedral Grove → Tofino
~130 km

Morning: Little Qualicum Falls — waterfalls, forest, swimming. Then Cathedral Grove — 800-year-old Douglas firs. Fuel in Port Alberni. Continue to Tofino. Evening on Chesterman Beach.

Day 21 · Fri 8 Aug
Tofino: Tide-Led Beach Day
~20 km local 🔒

Check tides the evening before. Low tide: Chesterman tombolo (walk to Frank Island) or Crystal Cove tide pools. Any tide: Long Beach, Rainforest Trail (1 km boardwalk). Surf lesson at Cox Bay.

Day 22 · Sat 9 Aug
Bear Watching & Ucluelet
~40 km 🔒

Morning: Bear-watching boat tour — black bears foraging on the shore. Afternoon: Ucluelet, Wild Pacific Trail Lighthouse Loop (2.6 km, stunning coast).

Day 23 · Sun 10 Aug
Tofino: Slow Day or Hot Springs Cove
varies

Option A: Tide-led slow day. Tonquin Beach, galleries, beach fire. South Chesterman for quiet time. Option B: Floatplane to Hot Springs Cove — natural hot springs on the wild coast. Full day.

🌊 Tofino Insider Tips

Check tides every evening. Chesterman tombolo at low tide. Crystal Cove for tide pools instead of crowded Long Beach. Use Tonquin or South Chesterman when the village core is busy.

Day 24 · Mon 11 Aug ⚠️
Tofino → Comox / Courtenay
~300 km

⚠️ Longest road day. Check DriveBC for Hwy 4. East on Hwy 4, north on Hwy 19. Lunch stop in Parksville or Qualicum Beach. Quiet evening in Comox Valley.

Day 25 · Tue 12 Aug
Cumberland, Bevan Ghost Town & Seal Bay
~30 km local

Morning: Cumberland — coal-mining history, one of BC's largest Chinatowns. Bevan ghost village nearby. Afternoon: Seal Bay Nature Park — forest trails to waterfront with seals and birds. Pair history with nature for the best day.

Day 26 · Wed 13 Aug ⚠️
Comox → Sunshine Coast (double ferry)
~60 km + 2 ferries 🔒

⚠️ Double-ferry day — build generous buffers. Ferry Comox → Powell River. Walk the historic townsite, lunch at Townsite Brewery. Drive to Saltery Bay. Ferry to Earls Cove (non-reservable — arrive early). Drive to Madeira Park.

5

Sunshine Coast / Family

13–17 Aug · 4 nights · Family time in Pender Harbour

Days 27–29 · Thu 14 – Sat 16 Aug
Family Time in Pender Harbour
local

Four slow days. Kayak Pender Harbour, swim in Katherine Lake and Hotel Lake. Pender Hill for harbour views (early or late for light). Francis Point Provincial Park — rocky coast and painted turtle habitat. Harbour cruise, local galleries. Skookumchuck Narrows optional — only if timed to tidal exchange.

Day 30 · Sun 17 Aug
Madeira Park → Vancouver
~88 km + ferry 🔒

Drive to Langdale (~58 km). Ferry to Horseshoe Bay (~40 min). Drive to Vancouver (~30 km). Check into apartment. Return rental car.

6

Vancouver Wind-Down

17–20 Aug · 3 nights · No agenda pressure

Day 31 · Mon 18 Aug
Relaxed Vancouver
0 km

Revisit favourites, last shopping, Kitsilano Beach saltwater pool. Museum of Anthropology at UBC. Ice cream on the seawall.

Day 32 · Tue 19 Aug
Final Day
0 km

Pack up. Last morning walk. Souvenirs, goodbyes.

Day 33 · Wed 20 Aug
Fly Out
—

Transfer to YVR. Fly home.
